Welcome to PVPSIT

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT), established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. Sponsored by the Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, PVPSIT offers cutting-edge education in engineering, technology, and management disciplines.

Located in Kanuru, Vijayawada, the college spans over **19.98 acres** with world-class infrastructure, including modern laboratories and a monolithic RCC structure. The campus ensures the academic and research needs of its students are met through excellent facilities.

PVPSIT is an **Autonomous Institution** approved by AICTE, affiliated to **JNTUK Kakinada**, and accredited with an **A+ grade by NAAC**. It offers undergraduate programs in various disciplines like CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, Civil Engineering, and postgraduate programs including M.Tech and MBA.

The college is also ISO 9001:2015 Certified and has been recognized in the **NIRF Rankings** for innovation. With a strong emphasis on holistic personality development, PVPSIT empowers students through research opportunities, innovation programs, and industry collaborations.

The highest placement package offered is **44 LPA**, and the institution has **MOUs with 60+ reputed industries** to bridge the gap between academics and industry.
