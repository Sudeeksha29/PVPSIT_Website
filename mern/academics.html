Academic Regulations

The college is an autonomous college affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada. As a consequence of the autonomy, the academic regulations in respect of the academic program of study are prepared and approved by the Academic Council, the highest academic body in the college.

View Regulations (PDF)

Curriculum & Syllabus

The curriculum and course syllabus for each program are created by the Boards of Studies and approved by the Academic Council. Each program is designed with specific Program Outcomes (skills, knowledge, and attitudes) and Program Educational Objectives in mind. The courses are structured to ensure they contribute to achieving these outcomes. The curriculum and syllabus may vary depending on the year a student was admitted.
